BusiTech Bootcamp is a collaborative program between HUST and FTU that nurtures new technology projects with practical applications and high value. Participants receive in-depth training and work together to build feasible projects with real-world value.

Why BusiTech?

Opportunity for practical application

BusiTech Bootcamp offers participants the opportunity to incubate new technology projects with practical applications that can create real-world value. Through the program, participants will receive training and work together to build feasible projects that can be implemented successfully.

In-depth training

The program provides in-depth training on both technical and business aspects, giving participants a comprehensive understanding of the process of building a successful tech product. Additionally, the program offers a networking day, where participants can exchange ideas and experiences with other like-minded individuals.

Mentorship and guidance

BusiTech Bootcamp offers mentorship and guidance from experienced industry professionals, who can provide valuable insights and advice on how to develop a successful tech product. The intensive bootcamp also provides an opportunity for mentor-led sessions to further refine and polish the projects.
